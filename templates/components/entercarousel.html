What is JoinOurStory?

JoinOurStory is an amazingly engaging soap opera that members can enjoy:

  • Regular video episodes.
    •
  • Live and in person.
    •
  • Anytime - 24/7 - online.
    •

Even better, JOS members can join the story and improve their lives through active partipation.

How does JOS work?

The JOS experience begins with our exciting show, from Emmy winning writers and starring veteran actors.

Enthusiastic members continue on our easy-to-use website: making friends, discussing the show and contributing new characters and stories.

Finally, members learn and participate at exhilarating live events, featuring performances by our JOS cast.

Most Importantly:

JoinOurStory members are continuously motivated and supported by our trained staff with in person events, online coaching and live help as they advance toward the ultimate reward: seeing their creations come to life.
